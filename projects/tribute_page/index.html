Elon Musk

Elon Musk, (Elon Reeve Must) is a business magnate and investor. He is the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of SpaceX, CEO and produce architect of Tesla, Inc with an estimated net worth of around $238 billion as of September 26, 2022.

  • Grew up in Pretoria, South Africa
  • Matriculated at Queen's University
  • 1995, confounded Zip2 and acquired for $307 million in 1999
  • 1999, cofounded X.com which merged with Confinity to form PayPal, which was bought by eBay for $ 1.5 billion
  • 2002, founded SpaceX, an aerospace manufacturer
  • 2004, invested in Tesla Motors, an electric vehicle manufacture
  • 2006, create SolarCity, a solar energy company which was later acquired by Tesla and renamed Tesla Energy
  • 2008, became Tesla Motors chairman, product architect, and CEO

In May 2002, Elon Musk founded SpaceX with an initial investement of $100 million from his personal fortune. He became the company's CEO and Chief Engineer

SpaceX attempted its first launch of the Falcon 1 rocket in 2006 and was awarded a Commercial Orbital Transportation Services program contract from NASA. After two failed attempts that nearly caused Must and his companies to bunkrupt. SpaceX succeeded in launching the Falcon 1 into orbit in 2008.

Tesla, Inc - originally Tesla Motors - was incorporated in 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning who financed the company until the Series A round of funding. Must led the Series A round of investment in February 2004 and invested $6.5 million becoming the majority shareholder, joined Tesla's board of directors as chairman.

Tesla first built an electric sports car, the Roadster, 2008. With sales of about 2500 vehicles, it was the first serial production all-electric car to use lithium-ion battery cells. Tesla began delivery of its four-door Model S sedan in 2012. A cross-over, the Model X was launched in 2015. A mass market sedan, the Model 3, was released in 2017. The Model 3 is the all-time bestselling plug-in electric car worldwide.
