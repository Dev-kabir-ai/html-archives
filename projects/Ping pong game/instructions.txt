Instructions For Ping Pong game:-

1.Enter will Start the game.
2.Player 1 can move left or right by using A And D keys.
3.Player 2 can move left or right by using <- and -> arrow keys.
4.After each game the player who loses will get the ball.
5.The player who loses his life 3 time will lose the Game.

I hope you will like the Game..!!
